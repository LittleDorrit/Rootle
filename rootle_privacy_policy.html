Privacy Policy for Rootle

Effective Date: June 30, 2025
Last Updated: June 30, 2025

Rootle ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Rootle mobile application (the "App"), which is designed to help users build biomedical English vocabulary through interactive learning.

This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

However, to improve our services and provide core functionality, we may collect the following information automatically:

  • App usage data (screens visited, time spent)
  • Crash logs and error reports
  • Subscription status and transaction history
  • Anonymous identifiers (such as device ID or Firebase Instance ID)

2. Data Collected via Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party tools to provide functionality and analytics:

Firebase (Google LLC)
Used for:

  • Real-time database access (content delivery)
  • Crash reporting and error logs
  • Analytics (anonymized)
  • Cloud storage (audio files)
Firebase may collect device type, OS version, country, and crash stack traces. Learn more

Google Play Billing / Apple In-App Purchases
Used for processing subscription payments. We do not see or store your credit card or personal billing information — this is handled securely by Google or Apple.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Understand how users engage with the content
  • Detect and fix bugs
  • Enable optional premium features (e.g., subscription access)

4. Data Retention and Security

We retain non-personal data as long as necessary to improve our services. All data is stored on secure, encrypted servers provided by Firebase. We do not sell or rent your data to any third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

Rootle is intended for students 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us at grammarsplaining@gmail.com.

6. Your Rights and Controls

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no account or profile to delete. However, if you have questions about your data or wish to report a concern, you may contact us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with a new “Effective Date.” Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of any changes.

8. Removing this app

Rootle does not collect any personally identifiable information from users. If you wish to stop using the app, you may simply uninstall it. No user account or data is stored.
